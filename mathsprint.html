How to play

  • Pick which operations to drill (+ − × ÷), a level, and a time limit.
  • Type each answer on the pad or your keyboard. Correct answers advance automatically.
  • Build a streak for consecutive correct answers; a wrong answer shows the solution and resets it.
  • Score as many as you can before time runs out — your best is saved.
