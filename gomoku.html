How to play

  • Take turns placing stones on the grid. The first to line up five in a row — horizontal, vertical or diagonal — wins.
  • vs Computer: you are black and move first against a threat-aware opponent. 2 Players: take turns on one device.
  • Undo takes back the last move (both moves against the computer).
  • The scoreboard tracks wins across rounds.
