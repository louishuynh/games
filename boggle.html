How to play

  • Build words of 3+ letters by tapping or swiping across touching tiles (including diagonals).
  • Tiles connect in a chain — you can't reuse a tile within one word.
  • Lift your finger after a swipe, tap the last tile again, or press Enter to submit; Clear or Esc starts over.
  • Scoring: 3–4 letters = 1, 5 = 2, 6 = 3, 7 = 5, 8+ = 11. The Qu tile counts as two letters.
