    How to play

    • Two teams take turns. One player is the clue-giver and holds the phone.
    • Describe the big word so your team guesses it — but you can't say the word itself or any of the forbidden words listed.
    • Got it +1 when they guess, Taboo −1 if you slip up (the other team is watching!), Skip to pass.
    • Get as many as you can before time runs out, then pass to the other team. First to the target score wins.
