Mastermind

Crack the hidden colour code by deduction.

How to play

  • A secret code of coloured pegs is hidden. You have 10 guesses to crack it.
  • Click a colour to fill the next slot (click a slot to select it, or to clear it). Press Guess when the row is full.
  • Feedback: a white peg = right colour and right spot; an outline peg = right colour, wrong spot.
  • Colours can repeat in the code. Use the clues to deduce the exact code.
