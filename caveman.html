How to play

  • Two teams take turns. One player is the clue-giver and can only describe the card using words of one syllable ("me want big cat with mane").
  • A single word scores 1 point; a phrase scores 3. Get as many as you can before time runs out.
  • If the clue-giver says a word with more than one syllable, the other team taps Bonk −1.
  • Swipe right (or tap Got it) when they guess it; swipe left (or Skip) to pass. First to the target score wins.
