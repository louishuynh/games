How to play

  • Tap a letter to move it between the tray and your answer, or drag letters to rearrange them.
  • You can also use a keyboard: type letters, Backspace to take one back, Enter to submit.
  • Use all the letters to spell a real word. Some jumbles have more than one answer.
  • Each solve scores points and builds your streak — beat the clock!
