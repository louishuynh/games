How to play

  • Rearrange the scrambled letters into a real word using all of them.
  • Some scrambles have more than one answer — any valid word counts.
  • Type your guess and press Enter. Shuffle re-jumbles the letters to help you see it.
  • Each solve scores points (longer words score more) and builds your streak. Beat the clock!
