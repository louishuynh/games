How to play

  • Slide a tile into the empty space by clicking it (any tile in the blank's row or column shifts along).
  • Use the arrow keys or swipe to slide tiles into the gap.
  • Arrange the numbers in order, 1 to 15, with the blank in the bottom-right.
  • Every scramble is guaranteed solvable. Tiles already home show green.
