How to play

  • Two teams take turns. One player is the drawer and secretly sees the word.
  • Draw it on the pad so your team guesses — no letters, numbers or words, and no talking!
  • Tap Got it +1 when they guess, or Skip for a new word. Use Peek to re-check the word privately.
  • Draw as many as you can before time runs out, then pass to the other team. First to the target wins.
