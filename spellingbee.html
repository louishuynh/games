How to play

  • Every word must be 4 letters or longer and use the centre letter.
  • Tap letters, swipe across them, or use the keyboard to build a word; press Enter to submit.
  • Letters may be reused as often as you like.
  • No puzzle ever contains the letter S.
  • Scoring: 4-letter words are worth 1 point; longer words score 1 point per letter; a pangram (uses all seven letters) scores +7.
  • Ranks climb from Beginner to Genius (70% of the maximum score).
