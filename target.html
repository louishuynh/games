How to play

  • Every word must be 4 letters or longer and use the centre letter.
  • Tap letters, swipe across them, or use the keyboard to build a word; press Enter to submit.
  • Use each letter no more than it appears in the grid — letters generally can't be repeated.
  • There is always at least one word that uses all nine letters.
  • No plurals or verb forms ending in S.
  • Pick Easy, Medium or Hard; ranks climb Good → Very good → Excellent.
