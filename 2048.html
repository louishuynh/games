How to play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD (or swipe on touch) to slide all tiles.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one worth double.
  • Every move adds a new 2 or 4 to a random empty square.
  • Reach the 2048 tile to win — then keep going for a higher score.
